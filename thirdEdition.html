NETWORK OF WOMEN


N.O.W. stands for Network of Women. This is an initiative by ThoughtWorks, and is a growing and connected community of women. ThoughtWorks N.O.W. is a forum for curious women from all walks of life, looking to be part of an open and inclusive network, to come together to learn from each other and be inspired. It provides a positive and motivating environment by bringing fresh content, wonderful speakers and trainers, and innovative formats.


N.O.W. brings together women from the entrepreneurial to the curious, ambitious and successful leaders. They have a passionate enthusiasm for sharing their choices, their journeys, successes and failures with the larger community of women. And this is the reason for N.O.W. to exist. Come, hear the experiences that have shaped the person, these strong women have become. Learn practical tips and ideas for putting your best and most confident foot forward. Do encourage other women in your network to come and join in the activities and network with us at N.O.W!

Speakers

Dr Madhumalti Agashe is a versatile entrepreneur who successfully pursued different fields in her career such as commerce , medicine & now nutrition and fitness.She is the proprietor and lead fitness coach of the highly successful 'Shubhangad Gym', a fitness centre by a woman for women. Over the last 20 years she has helped more than 6000 members achieve their goals of fitness. Now at the age of 70 she is still going strong with no signs of slowing down

Rachna Rego, Director at Right Horizons has  25 years of rich experience in the corporate world. She joined Right Horizons in 2008 and has worked across various functions and department handling  Research,  financial planning , Training , Compliance , Quality, Process and IT implementations. She currently heads the Sales and Operations  function at Right Horizons, managing a diverse team spread across three cities - Bangalore, Chennai and Hyderabad. Known for her infectious laugh, her other interests include Reading, Travelling and loves meeting new people.

Niharica is a Psychologist and Facilitator of Art and Movement Therapies. Her passion lies in creating safe spaces for individuals and groups to explore, express and transform themselves and their relationships. She is a firm believer of self-compassion and self-care is an irreplaceable component of emotional first-aid.
